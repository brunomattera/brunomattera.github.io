Approach

How I think about projects

Design is not just about how things look. These are the principles I come back to, regardless of the project or the brief.

01

Listen before designing

I start by understanding the real problem, not the assumed one. Research, conversations, and context come before any sketch.

02

Design with intention

Every decision has a reason. I don't place a pixel without purpose, each choice is tied back to a user need or a business goal.

03

Test without fear

I put work in front of users early and often. Being wrong fast is better than being wrong at launch, iteration is how good ideas become great ones.

04

Build collaboratively

Great design doesn't happen in isolation. I coach, align, and co-create across disciplines, with developers, PMs, and stakeholders at every stage.

05

Sweat the details

From system architecture to icon alignment, the last 10% is where trust is built. Polish isn't vanity, it's the part users feel even when they can't name it.
